Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2025

This privacy policy explains how the Hidden Camera Detector app handles your data.

Data Collection

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data.

All detection processes (e.g., camera scan, Bluetooth device scan, magnetometer readings) are performed locally on your device.

Third-party Services

This app does not use third-party services such as analytics, ads, or cloud data storage.

Permissions

  • Camera access (if required): used strictly for visual inspection features.
  • Bluetooth and sensor access: used only for detecting nearby hardware and analyzing magnetic fields.
